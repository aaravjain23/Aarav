My Story

I didn't start my journey wanting to predict markets — I started by trying to understand why they move.

During my undergraduate years at NMIMS, I was fascinated by how data could reveal market behaviour long before narratives caught up. I built my first financial model with more curiosity than confidence, and realised that numbers tell stories — if you learn to listen.

But curiosity alone wasn't enough. Early on, I faced the gap between theory and market reality — where valuations met volatility, and models met uncertainty. I learned that mastering finance meant mastering data, and that's where I turned to AI, Python, and analytics to bring structure to complexity.

Each project — from building ESG-integrated Smart Beta portfolios to optimising risk using derivatives and scenario modelling — taught me that finance today is as much about algorithms as judgment.

Now, as an MSc Finance candidate at NUS, I'm continuing that journey — exploring how machine learning, quantitative finance, and behavioural insights can converge to shape better investment decisions.
